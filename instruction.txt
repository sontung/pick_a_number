Choose which number to choose from the list displayed.
You can only choose the first and the last number of the
list by the first and last button on the screen.
Each number you choose will be added to your score.
Player who has the highest score is the winner.
Remember the AI is designed to beat you so decide your
strategy wisely and I wish you good luck.
Btw, you can always press Esc button in your keyboard
if you want to quit the game
